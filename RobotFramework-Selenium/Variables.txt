## SCOPE of Variables ###
## Variables can be GLOBAL accessible to entire project or SUITE and TEST CASES variable accessible only for a specific *** test cases ***
## keyword variables are test only *** variables ***
## name convention is to use upper case letter for global variables &{BROWSER} and lower case for other variables ${my_data}

*** Keywords ***
# you can create keyward variables with arguments and pass them at runtime
Begin Web Test1
    [Arguments]      ${url}    ${browser}
    Open browser     ${url}    ${browser}[1]

Begin Web Test2
    [Arguments]      @{url_browser}
    Open browser     @{url_browser}[0]    @{url_browser}[1]
